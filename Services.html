Services

Pet sitting & drop-ins (most popular!) Rate
Away for a few hours and have an anxious pet, going on vacation, or just getting away for the weekend? I have you covered.
With during the day or overnight pet sitting options, your pet will have the constant care it needs.
Pet sitting includes house sitting and home care, with services such as light cleaning, plant watering, and mail gathering free of charge.
Drop ins are also available for just a quick feeding, let outside, or other quick tasks, but do not include long walks.

Overnight pet sitting rate: $50/day

Drop in rate: $20/ drop in
 
Dog Walking Rate
Do you have a busy schedule but want to help get your furry friends wiggles out? Regular walks are a great option!
I can take your pet around your neighborhood for a nice breath of fresh are and potty break.
Walks are also a great alternative to a drop-in if you would like a feeding and a walk afterward.

Starting rate: $25 for 30 min

Longer than 30 min up to an hour: $35 (Good for big dogs with lots of energy!)
 
Other Needs Rate
  1. Do you have an exotic pet in need of specialized care? Contact for a quote and if it is something I can do!
  2. Does your pet have a vet visit or grooming appointment while you are away or during a pet sit? I can take them there for a small travel fee.
  3. Forget to buy food or need me to grab something from the store? I can grab that for you for a small travel fee and the total cost of the items.
  4. Are you an existing client and Get something special delivered while you are away? I’m happy to bring the item inside for you when not scheduled to pet sit. (Existing clients only, depending on availability.)
