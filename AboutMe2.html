About Audrey

Audrey is a college student and part time worker located in DuPont, Washington. With a love for all animals including dogs, cats, and reptiles, she strives to provide the best care and make you and your pets happy.
She has been doing various pet services for over 8 years consistently, including working with small dogs, very large dogs, dogs needing medication, animals with anxiety, and much more.
Audrey is confident that she can handle any task you may need for your furry family member. Audrey specializes in dog care, and has experience with cats, reptiles, and rodents.

Contact her with any questions regarding extra care you may need!
